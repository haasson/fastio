<objective>
Deliver the OPS-03 vertical slice: a working /terms page reachable from the storefront footer of every tenant. The slice is end-to-end thin — one new page, one footer modification, one unit test for the gate logic. After this plan, a real customer on any tenant with complete legalInfo can click "Оферта" in the footer and read the offer; tenants with incomplete legalInfo see no broken link and no exposed empty page outside of direct URL access.

Purpose: Close the legal-documents gap so each tenant has both a Privacy Policy and a Terms of Service (Оферта) available at predictable URLs, satisfying basic Russian e-commerce requirements (152-ФЗ + ГК §437) for live customer ordering.

- New page `apps/storefront/pages/terms.vue` (mirror of privacy.vue, food-delivery offer text in 6 §-sections, header with tenant legalInfo)
